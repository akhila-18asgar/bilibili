YouTube channel logos by Canva

Growing your channel is a mix of hard work, strategy, and creativity. Apart from producing interesting, high-quality video content, you have to establish your brand with a memorable channel name and an eye-catching, custom YouTube channel logo.

Cross the logo off your list—with Canva’s free YouTube channel logo templates, you’ll make a logo in minutes. Browse our collection of premade layouts and pick one that will help you make the best logo design for your YouTube channel. You can use the template as a source of inspiration if you’re creating from scratch. Or you can use it as a base and customize it to your liking. To begin, sort the free logo templates for YouTube channels using our filters. You can filter results according to themes like gaming, fashion, and music, or styles like modern or retro.

Found the perfect template? Customize it on our intuitive design dashboard using our beginner-friendly editing tools. You can drag and drop graphic design elements like vectors, illustrations, and stock photos onto your layout. Tip: use icons and graphics related to your content, so that at first glance, viewers know what to expect from you and your channel. For music producers, check out our illustrations of soundwaves and headsets. For beauty channels, there are makeup elements in the library. There are also illustrations of workout equipment for fitness channels, consoles for gamers and streamers, and book icons for educational channels.

You can even rope in your co-creator in the logo-making process. Send them a link to the logo design for your YouTube channel and make them a co-collaborator. Once finished, download it in the format you need and upload it to your YouTube channel. Feel free to repurpose the logo, too. Resize the dimensions and use it as profile pictures on your social media accounts.
